Watching Pornography and Hentai is Haram

In Islam, every action that encourages immorality, indecency, and lust is considered haram (forbidden). Pornography and hentai violate the teachings of Islam by promoting explicit and immoral content, corrupting the heart and soul, and leading believers away from the path of righteousness. These forms of entertainment cause harm to individuals and society by normalizing sinful behavior and fostering addiction.

Surah An-Nur (24:30)
وَقُل لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَغُضُّوا مِنْ أَبْصَارِهِمْ وَيَحْفَظُوا فُرُوجَهُمْ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ أَزْكَىٰ لَهُمْ ۗ إِنَّ اللَّهَ خَبِيرٌ بِمَا يَصْنَعُونَ

"Tell the believing men to lower their gaze and guard their private parts. That is purer for them. Verily, Allah is All-Aware of what they do."

Surah An-Nur (24:19)
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يُحِبُّونَ أَن تَشِيعَ الْفَاحِشَةُ فِي الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ فِي الدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ ۚ وَاللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ وَأَنتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ

"Indeed, those who like that immorality should spread among those who have believed will have a painful punishment in this world and the Hereafter. And Allah knows, while you know not."

Surah Al-Isra (17:32)
وَلَا تَقْرَبُوا الزِّنَىٰ إِنَّهُ كَانَ فَاحِشَةً وَسَاءَ سَبِيلًا

"And do not approach unlawful sexual intercourse. Indeed, it is ever an immorality and is evil as a way."

Islam promotes chastity and self-control to avoid falling into the trap of sinful temptations. Watching indecent content not only damages the viewer’s spirituality but also attracts the wrath of Allah (SWT). We are accountable for our actions, and every small deed, including what we watch and consume, will be weighed on the Day of Judgment.

May Allah (SWT) guide us on the right path, protect us from all forms of temptation, and grant us strength to stay firm in our faith. Ameen.
